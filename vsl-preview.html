Opening Hook (0-30 seconds)

"475,000 people follow @thestoicbiohacker. Most of them will never become patients. Not because they are not interested - because there is no system that routes them from content consumer to the right offer at the right time. That is the problem. This is the plan to fix it."

Section 1 - The Observation (30s-2 min)

"The Longevity Clinic is already operating at a clinical standard that very few organisations can match. Four geographies. GMP-certified stem cell and peptide protocols. Yale-trained physicians. A neuropathologist as Medical Director. The clinical infrastructure is built.

The marketing front-end has not caught up.

Here is what I mean: the funnel currently runs like this. Content - 475,000 followers watching on Instagram. Then there is a gap. Then Elite Program intake. Everything in the middle - the warm, interested, not-yet-ready audience - has no home.

Three things are missing. I am going to walk through all three."

Section 2 - Bottleneck 1: The capture problem (2-4 min)

"First: there is no email capture mechanism between the content and the Elite Program.

A Longevity Audit quiz - three to five questions, personalised output on the other side, email capture required - sits in that gap. A follower who sees a stoic-biohacker reel about peptide therapy, wants more, but is not yet ready for a clinical assessment has somewhere to go. They opt in. They get a personalised result. They are segmented by readiness.

This builds a list. The list feeds the cohort waitlist and the Elite Program intake. It is the front gate that is currently open with no one manning it."

Section 3 - Bottleneck 2: The MT cohort (4-7 min)

"Second: the answer to the question Noah raised in July 2025 - how does this work for mental training and coaching?

The Stoic Performance Protocol. 8-week cohort. 25-seat cap. Weekly group call. Curriculum: mindset profiling, peak-state protocols, stoic frameworks for executive performance, precision habit design. Price point $2,000-$5,000 per seat. Four cohorts per year.

This is not another course. It is a structured protocol for the people who already trust Noah's framework but are not yet positioned for Elite Program pricing. And every cohort graduate becomes a pre-qualified prospect for clinical intake.

The cohort scales the MT offer independent of clinic capacity. It answers the question from July 2025. It builds the bridge."

Section 4 - Bottleneck 3: The unified front door (7-9 min)

"Third: @thestoicbiohacker and The Longevity Clinic operate as two separate digital surfaces. A warm Instagram follower who wants to go deeper has to independently make the connection between the personal brand and the clinic.

A unified hub bridges them. One surface, one voice, one routing system. Content followers land in a place that speaks their language and shows them the path to the right tier of intake - whether that is the cohort, the quiz, or the Elite Program."

Proof Section (9-10 min)

"Our best client went from zero to $172,000 per month in our first month. That was an executive-facing B2B services firm. Closest comp to TLC's positioning. We built the front-end. They delivered the service. That is the model.

All three builds - quiz, cohort landing page, unified hub - are live within seven days of kickoff."

Close (10-12 min)

"No pitch on the call. We walk the plan, you decide. If it is the right fit, we start. If it is not, you have a full plan you can take anywhere.

Book a call at the link below."
